Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled eggs are some of the easiest food to make, but they can be quite difficult to get perfect.

In this recipe, I will show you how to make the best scrambled eggs in just five minutes!

Ingredients

You will need:

  • 3 eggs,
  • milk or water - milk will give you a creamier texture, but water works fine,
  • butter,
  • salt and pepper.

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs into a medium bowl, and whisk until the yolk and whites are combined.
  2. Pour in a quarter cup of milk, and whisk again! The eggs should be an even yellow color, with no spots or streaks.
  3. Gently preheat the pan, and melt a small knob of butter in it.
  4. Pour the egg mixture in and let it cook for a few seconds, undisturbed.
  5. Pull a spatula across the bottom of the pan to form curds of eggs.
  6. Continue cooking, making sure to scrape the sides of the pan so that the eggs do not dry out.
  7. When the eggs are mostly set, remove the pan from heat.
  8. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Now you have perfect scrambled eggs!
