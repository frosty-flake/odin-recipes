Homemade Lemonade

Description

Lemonade is fun, refreshing, and easy to make! You probably already have the ingredients. Let's make a lemonade today!

Ingredients

You will need:

  • Sugar and water
  • Lemons
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Roll your lemons on the countertop to get more juice. Then, cut them in half and juice them, either by hand or with a juicer.
  2. Combine sugar and water in a saucepan, bring the mixture to a boil, and stir until the sugar is dissolved to make syrup.
  3. Pour cold water into a pitcher. Stir in lemon juice and pulp, and add the sugar syrup to taste
  4. Serve in a glass with ice.
